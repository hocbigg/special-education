# Summary

The Special Education curriculum is a **complete education in Special Education** using online materials.

Note: When there are courses or books that don't fit into the curriculum but are otherwise of high quality,
they belong in [extras/courses](extras/courses.md), [extras/readings](extras/readings.md) or [extras/other_curricula](extras/other_curricula.md).

# Curriculum

| Subject | Why study? | Book | Videos |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Foundations of Special Education | Understand the historical, legal, and philosophical basis for special education to ensure ethical and compliant practices. | *Exceptional Children: An Introduction to Special Education* by William L. Heward | [Teaching Students with Special Needs](https://alison.com/course/working-with-students-with-special-educational-needs-revised) |
| Characteristics of Learners with Disabilities | Identify and comprehend various disabilities to provide appropriate support and interventions. | *Children with Disabilities* by Mark L. Batshaw | [Exploring Learning Disabilities: Supporting Belonging](https://www.open.edu/openlearn/health-sports-psychology/exploring-learning-disabilities-supporting-belonging) |
| Assessment and Individualized Education Programs (IEPs) | Learn to evaluate student needs and create effective IEPs for personalized learning plans. | *Assessment in Special Education: A Practical Approach* by Roger Pierangelo | [The Self-Determined IEP: Supporting Success for Students with Behavioural Difficulties and Social Maladjustments](https://www.ulaval.ca/en/academics/mooc-massive-open-online-courses/the-self-determined-iep-supporting-success-for-students-with-behavioural-difficulties-and-social-maladjustments) |
| Instructional Strategies in Special Education | Acquire adapted teaching methods to promote academic success for students with diverse needs. | *Strategies for Teaching Students with Learning and Behavior Problems* by Sharon R. Vaughn | [Evidence-Based Instructional Practices Overview](https://promotingprogress.org/resource-collections/online-learning-courses) |
| Behavior Management in Special Education | Develop techniques to handle challenging behaviors and foster positive classroom environments. | *The Special Educator's Guide to Behavior Management* by Paul Mooney | [Providing Social, Emotional, Behavioral, and Special Education Services in School](https://www.coursera.org/learn/social-services-education) |
| Inclusive Education and Collaboration | Promote integration in general education settings and effective teamwork with families and professionals. | *Teaching Students with Special Needs in Inclusive Settings* by Tom E. C. Smith | [Disability Inclusion in Education: Building Systems of Support](https://www.coursera.org/learn/disability-inclusion-education) |
